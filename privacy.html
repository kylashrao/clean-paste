Privacy Policy


At Clean-Paste, accessible from our Vercel web app, your data privacy is our top priority. We do not collect, process, or store any text or links you paste into our tools.


How Data is Processed

All data manipulation (clearing telemetry markers and formatting strings) occurs fully client-side using JavaScript in your own browser tab. No clipboard details are transmitted over the web.


Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google uses cookies (such as the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other internet platforms. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
