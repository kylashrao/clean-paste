About Clean-Paste


Clean-Paste was launched in 2026 to solve a daily frustration: bloated URLs and messy text formatting. Our mission is to provide lightning-fast web utilities that prioritize absolute privacy.


Unlike other platforms, we don't store your logs, we don't ask for user registration, and we don't process your inputs on a remote server. Everything runs directly inside your local web browser.
